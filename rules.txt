# 📜 Server Rules

## Welcome to Trans Youth India 🏳️‍⚧️ 🏳️‍🌈 

The official Discord server of r/TransYouthIndia.
This is a supportive, youth-centered space for trans, nonbinary, and questioning people in India.

Please follow these rules to keep the community safe and welcoming for everyone.

1️⃣ Respect all identities

> No transphobia, queerphobia, casteism, misogyny, ableism, racism, or discrimination of any kind. Misgendering, deadnaming, harassment, or invalidating someone’s identity will not be tolerated.

2️⃣ This space centers trans youth in India

> This server is primarily for trans, nonbinary, and gender-questioning people roughly ages 13–30 in India. Allies may join and support, but conversations should remain centered on the trans community.

3️⃣ No hate, harassment, or gatekeeping

> No “real trans” debates or identity policing.
> Transmedicalist arguments, anti-DIY harassment, or invalidating others’ transitions are not allowed.
> Everyone’s journey is valid: questioning, binary, nonbinary, stealth, visible, or still figuring things out.

4️⃣ No NSFW content

> This is a strictly SFW server.
> Sexual content, explicit images, fetish discussions, or chaser behavior will result in removal or bans. Only limited sexual content is allowed in <#1400479289006227598> 

5️⃣ Respect boundaries & no unsolicited DMs

> Do not DM people without asking first.
> If someone says no, respect it.
> Creepy behavior, flirting with minors, or chaser behavior will result in immediate bans.

6️⃣ Use the correct channels

> Please post in the appropriate channels to keep things organized.

> Examples:
